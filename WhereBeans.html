How to Buy the Best Coffee Beans



Buy local if you can. You are not only supporting your community but also the beans will usually be a better quality. By buying local, these beans will mostly be freshly roasted will will improve it's aroma, leading to a better coffee.

Always buy whole beans. This is one of the most important things to remember. Pre-ground beans may seem like a good idea but you are losing alot of flavour if you do so.

Air is coffee's enemy. Once you open your brand new bag of beautiful coffee, make sure you grind then brew as fast as possible.

Check the roast date. Although old beans wont kill you, fresh beans will make you immortal so buy fresh.
